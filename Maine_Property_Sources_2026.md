## NOTES FOR MONITORING

1. **Banks**: Most banks don't publicly list REO properties; contact REO departments directly
2. **Government Properties**: Check county/municipal websites regularly for tax foreclosure listings
3. **FSBO Sites**: Many FSBO platforms syndicate to major sites (Zillow, Realtor.com)
4. **Social Media**: Facebook groups and Nextdoor require joining local communities
5. **Auction Sites**: Properties often listed weeks/months before auction dates
6. **MLS Access**: Some platforms require realtor access; others provide public portals

---

## RECOMMENDED MONITORING PRIORITY

### High Priority (Most Likely Pre-Zillow Listings)
1. FSBO platforms (Ownerama, Brokerless, etc.)
2. Local classifieds (Oodle, Town-Ads)
3. Facebook Marketplace & Groups
4. Nextdoor
5. Bank REO departments (direct contact)
6. Government tax foreclosure listings
7. Auction houses (EstateSale.com, GoToAuction.com)
8. Investment/wholesale platforms

### Medium Priority (May Have Early Listings)
1. Maine Listings (official MLS)
2. Local brokerages (may list before syndication)
3. Credit unions
4. Commercial real estate sites (for multi-family)

### Lower Priority (Aggregated/Syndicated)
1. Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin (typically aggregated from MLS)
2. Land-specific sites (if not looking for land)
